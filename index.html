This Is Not Real

This is not real – it is a piece of satire. This is a terrible business model that would be unethical in a number of ways. `Disgrace insurance` is already a concept in the film industry, but it is purchased on part of the studio and often packaged with other kinds of film insurance by default so as not to insinuate disgrace on part of the actors or producers. "Cancellation" insurance would be a product marketed toward petty C-listers who feel the need to hedge bets either against themselves or against what they perceive as an ignorant public. Now that I’ve thoroughly deflated the joke I’d like to give credit to the `Blue Check Homes` viral art project for inspiring this. This project is in no ways associated with `Blue Check Homes` or its creator.
